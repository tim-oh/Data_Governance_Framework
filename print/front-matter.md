# A data governance framework for digital social protection systems

**Guidance for practitioners, policymakers and system administrators**

2025

---

**Digital Convergence Initiative (DCI)**

DCI is the global initiative for the digital transformation of social protection systems. Established as part of the USP2030 partnership, the DCI is an open and collaborative platform for governments, development partners, civil society organizations and the private sector united by a shared vision: expanding the coverage of social protection and enhancing its delivery through inclusive, interoperable digital systems.

Visit our website at [spdci.org](https://spdci.org)
