# Organisational measures

Organisational measures are the policies, processes, and governance structures that manage security risks at the institutional level — ensuring that technical controls are supported by sound management practices.

## What good looks like

> _Placeholder: from framework text — tested incident response and business continuity plans, managed supplier risk, regular security reviews with documented findings_
