# Use, sharing and publication

The use, sharing, and publication of data is a critical stage in the data lifecycle. In this framework, it is addressed comprehensively under **Pillar 3: Access**, which covers the policies, mechanisms, and roles for internal use, external sharing, and open data publication.

See [Pillar 3: Access](../access/README.md) for full guidance.

## Why this is handled under Access

Data use and sharing involves a distinct set of governance challenges — around authorisation, data protection, and interoperability — that cut across all four pillars. Consolidating this guidance under the Access pillar avoids duplication and provides a single point of reference for practitioners managing data sharing arrangements.
